1. Introduction

GPS AI ("we", "our", or "us") provides an AI-powered platform to help users manage and run Meta (Facebook/Instagram) advertising campaigns. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Facebook/Meta account data: When you connect your Meta account, we access your Ad Account ID, campaign data, ad performance metrics, and Pages you manage — only with your explicit permission.
  • Usage data: We collect information about how you interact with GPS AI, including commands sent, campaigns created, and features used.
  • Contact information: Your email address if you sign up or contact us.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create, manage, and optimize your Meta ad campaigns on your behalf
  • To provide AI-generated insights and recommendations for your ads
  • To display your campaign performance and analytics
  • To improve our service and fix technical issues

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Meta Platforms: To execute ad operations via the Meta Ads API
  • Anthropic (Claude AI): To process your natural language instructions — no personally identifiable information is sent

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time (see Section 7). Ad performance data may be retained in anonymized form for up to 12 months for service improvement.

6. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) and secure token storage to protect your Meta access tokens and account data. We never store your Meta password.

7. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time. To request deletion, visit our Data Deletion page or email us at saadsumra36@gmail.com.

8. Facebook Login

GPS AI uses Facebook Login to authenticate users. By connecting your Facebook account, you agree to Meta's Data Policy. You can revoke GPS AI's access at any time from your Facebook Settings > Apps and Websites.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.
